A live demo of my first game.

For best expirience play on Chrome browser RULES The small circle on the bottom right corner is the controller. Find the next circle. Every turn the next circle wil reveal itself. Connect next circle. Try not to hit the following:

  • Node that is not the current goal.
  • Lines left by the triangle.
  • The bounadries. They will cost you one life. You have 10 lives.

Circles are generated in random positions.

This is alpha gameplay. Some bugs are still there or performance is not very good.
